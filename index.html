About Me

My name is Lauren Pinner and I am currently a student at Florida Polytechnic University working towards my Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering. I am from Lakeland, Florida, and have lived here my whole life.

Being from Florida, I love nature and I enjoy doing anything outside. In my free time, I fish, tend to the garden, and run. My love for the outdoors shaped my dream career, which is hopefully someday working to develop and improve sustainable energy sources. The future of the Earth is an important topic to me, and I want to do my part in caring for the environment.

Aside from that, music is a huge part of my life - everything from playing instruments to writing my own compositions. I mainly play the trumpet and have been for 7 years. If my schedule allows, I also want to play professionally part-time in an orchestra or concert band.
